Redistribution of Share Money

Innovision has made a profit in the Trade Fair, which is excellent because very few JA Companies actually manage to make a profit.

For this reason, Innovision has decided to redistribute the money back to the shareholders. For every share you have bought, you get HK$3 extra in return. This represents a 6% return on your investment in a 3 month period.

Every shareholder will be invited to our Annual General Meeting (AGM) in March where we will review our company operations, distribute our annual report and share what we have learnt.
